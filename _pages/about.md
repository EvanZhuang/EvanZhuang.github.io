Hey, I am Yufan! I am a Research Engineer at [IBM T. J. Watson Research Center](http://www.research.ibm.com/labs/watson/). I primarily work in graphical neural networks and natural language processing, sometimes wanders into other areas including computational sociology.

My current primary research goal is to develop a robust, explainable, generalizable deep learning framework for graph classification/generation with graphical neural networks. The application scenarios are mostly in C program vulnerability detection. I also have done work in data bias exploration and probabilistic topic modeling with applications in sociology.

I received Master of Science in Data Science from the [Data Science Institute](https://datascience.columbia.edu/) of Columbia University in 2020, also was a graduate research intern at IBM Research in the summer of 2019. I received my BSc. with First Class Honors from Hong Kong Polytechnic University with a major in Applied Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science in 2018.
